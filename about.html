About

Hey, I’m Chirag. I rather call me a learning photographer & filmmaker focused more on travel. I never miss an opportunity to tell a story through my photography and my videos. On one side I create websites and Apps and on the other, I share all my photography knowledge and adventures on this site.

Final word – I hope my art inspires people to pursue what they are most passionate about, to be fearless in their pursuit and to know that anything is possible. Seriously thank you to everyone who has supported my journey, I wouldn’t be doing what I’m doing if it wasn’t for you. So please follow me on Instagram and Flickr and give some love
